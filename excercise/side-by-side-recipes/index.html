Banana Bread Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 15 minutes.

Total Time: 25 minutes. Yields: 6.

Fluffy, cozy, and full of banana bread flavor, these pancakes are the perfect weekend treat. The edges get golden and buttery while the centers stay soft and full of melty chocolate chips. Stack them high, drizzle with maple syrup, and watch them disappear in seconds.
